# AI-Driven Fitness Coach

**A dynamically adaptive, data-driven fitness and wellness assistant.**

---

The AI-Driven Fitness Coach uses the Google Gemini API to provide highly personalized exercise and nutrition planning.
The app's goal is to offer an affordable and widely accessible alternative to traditional, expensive personal training services, while minimizing the risk of injury and maximizing user results.

The system continuously analyzes individual performance, logged data, and user feedback. As a result, plans are adapted in real-time, avoiding the rigidity of static apps and the risk of overtraining.
